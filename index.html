The problem
Finding internships shouldn't feel like a full-time job.

The system isn't built for students.

Existing platforms like LinkedIn and Handshake are designed for corporate HR teams and experienced professionals. Students — especially school students — are an afterthought.

Professors with open research spots have no easy way to reach motivated students. Small companies want intern talent but don't have recruiters. Everyone loses.

LaunchPad fixes this.
